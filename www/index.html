Salon Model Explorer

Game Theoretic Model of Firm Organization

Welcome to the Salon Model Explorer! This application allows you to explore a game theoretic model of firm organization, specialization, and productivity based on "The Inner Beauty of Firms" by Jacob Kohlhepp.

Features

  • Examine how economic shocks affect productivity and specialization
  • Compare reallocation vs. reorganization equilibria
  • Explore wage effects across worker skill sets
  • Visualize results with interactive plots

About the Model

The model examines how firms assign tasks to workers in a market equilibrium. It shows that task specialization within firms is related to productivity differences across firms, and that these differences are not explained by establishment size.

For more information, see the About tab in the application or refer to the original paper.

Based on "The Inner Beauty of Firms" by Jacob Kohlhepp (2025)
